2)

  1. How often do you use the microwave?
    • At least once a week
  2. Why do you use a microwave?
    • It is more convenient to heat up food than using a stove
  3. Did you know you had to press timer/clock before turning the knob to select a time?
    • No, the users assumed that turning the knob would allow them to select a time longer than 1 min
  4. Do you ever use the extra buttons like defrost/popcorn?
    • No, because users usually follow packaging instructions
  5. Do you know how to use these buttons?
    • No, because users never use them
  6. How do you decide how long to microwave things for?
    • From previous experience
    • If a new thing is being microwaved, users will start with one minute and continue doing one minute increments until it is warmed up to their liking
  7. Do you prefer the knob or the number pad on a microwave?
    • Number pad because more convenient and less confsuing
      • Cuts out irrelevant timing (no one is going to microwave something for 28 seconds instead of 30 seconds)
